## 🪟 Step 1 — Open the Terminal on Your Windows Laptop

1. Press the **Windows key** on your keyboard (the key with the ⊞ logo)
2. Type **`cmd`** in the search bar
3. Click on **Command Prompt** to open it

You'll see a dark window with some white text. That's the terminal. Don't be scared of it! 😄

---

## 😟 Something went wrong?

Here are the most common issues and how to fix them:

**"Connection refused" or "Connection timed out"**
→ Check your internet connection and try again.

**"Permission denied"**
→ Double-check the password. Make sure there are no extra spaces when typing it.

**The screen looks broken or has weird symbols**
→ Try making your Command Prompt window bigger, or zoom out a little.

**Nothing is happening after I type the password**
→ Just press Enter. It's invisible by design. 😊
